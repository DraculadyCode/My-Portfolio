About Me

My Education I graduated from the National School of Practical Engineers at the Technion (Israeli Institute of Technology) in Haifa. In 2017, I received my diploma in Software Practical Engineering.
This foundational education provided me with a solid understanding of software development principles, problem-solving skills, and a passion for technology.
In 2018, I moved to Germany to seek new opportunities and pursue a long-term career in web development.
I am currently a student at the Digital Career Institute (DCI) , studying Web Development, and I am also taking a German language course at the C1 level. This dual focus allows me to strengthen my technical skills while enhancing my communication abilities in a new cultural environment.

Current Status I am based in Berlin, I am set to complete my studies as a web developer in February 2025.
My education at DCI has exposed me to a wide range of web technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks.
I have learned frameworks such as React, Tailwind CSS, and Bootstrap, which have enabled me to develop interactive and responsive web applications.

Skills and Passion I have a strong passion for front-end development and design.
I enjoy creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that provide seamless user experiences.
My skills include responsive web design, UI/UX principles, and working with tools like Canva and PicsArt.
I am always eager to learn and explore new technologies to stay updated in this fast-paced industry.
I invite you to review my resume for a comprehensive overview of my qualifications and experiences.
Work Eligibility and Future Goals As a Portuguese citizen, an EU member state, I am eligible to work immediately without needing additional documents or visas.
Upon completing my Weiterbildung at DCI in February 2025, I will be seeking either a two-month internship or a permanent position to begin my professional journey.
I am particularly interested in roles that focus on front-end development, where I can contribute my skills while learning from experienced professionals.
Additionally, I am open to learning new technologies and techniques related to web development to enhance my skill set and adapt to the evolving industry.
