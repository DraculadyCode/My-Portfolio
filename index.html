About Me

I graduated from the National School of Practical Engineers at the Technion (Israeli Institute of Technology) in Haifa. In 2017, I received my diploma in Software Practical Engineering.
In 2018, I moved to Germany to seek new opportunities and pursue a long-term career.
I am currently a student at the Digital Career Institute (DCI) , studying Web Development, and I am also taking a German language course at the C1 level.
I am based in Berlin, I am set to complete my studies as a web developer in February 2025.
I have a strong passion for front-end development and design and am eager to start my career in this field.
As a Portuguese citizen, an EU member state, I am eligible to work immediately without needing additional documents or visas.
Upon completing my Weiterbildung at DCI in February 2025, I will be seeking either a two-month internship or a permanent position to begin my professional journey.
